About

AutoBio was established in 2020 to bring light, and knowledge of the electric car industry to the world. We strive to bring our readers the most accurate information, and best news possible. The inevitability of the car industry changing to full electric excites us! With new technologies advancing everyday, our goal is to spread the word.

To make things even better, we've added a new feature that allows our readers to search YouTube for anything and everything car related, right here on site! So if you happen to not see what you're looking for... We've got you covered.
